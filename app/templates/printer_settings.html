ðŸ’¡ Finding Your Camera URL:

FlashForge cameras often use these patterns:

  • http://PRINTER_IP:8080/?action=stream (MJPEG stream)
  • http://PRINTER_IP:8080/?action=snapshot (Single image)
  • http://PRINTER_IP:8899/camera (Some models)

Try opening these URLs in your browser while on the same network.
