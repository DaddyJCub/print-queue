🎁 What are credits?

Credits are a reward currency in Printellect. They give you ways to get prints, skip the queue, or grab store items without needing a card. Think of them as tokens you can redeem for various printing services.

✨ How do I earn credits?

👤

Admin grants

The admin can award credits to your account at any time as a reward or perk.

📅

Monthly auto-grants

Some users receive a monthly credit allowance that's automatically added to their balance.

🔄

Refunds

If a credit-purchased request is cancelled, your credits are refunded.

🛒 What can I spend credits on?

🏪

Store items

Items in the store with a credit price can be purchased using your credits instead of a card.

🚀

Rush priority

Skip the queue by paying the rush fee with credits instead of card payment.

🖨️

Custom print requests

Submit a custom print request using credits so you don't need a card on file.

❓ FAQs

Do credits expire?
No, credits don't expire. They stay in your account until you use them.
Can I buy credits?
Credits can't be purchased directly. They're granted by the admin as rewards or perks.
Where can I see my balance?
Your credit balance is on your profile page, along with a full transaction history.
What if I don't have enough credits?
You can always use card payment instead. Credits are an alternative, not a requirement. If an item supports both, you'll see both options.
