# Setup My Printellect Base

## 1) Power on
Power on your Printellect base.

## 2) Connect to setup Wi-Fi
On your phone, open Wi-Fi settings and join:

`PRINTELLECT-SETUP-xxxx`

`xxxx` is the last 4 characters of your device ID.

## 3) Open local setup page
In your phone browser, open:

`http://192.168.4.1/`

Enter:
- claim code (printed on label/QR)
- home Wi-Fi SSID
- home Wi-Fi password

Tap **Save and Reboot**.

## 4) Claim in Printellect
Log in to Printellect and claim your device by scanning the device QR (or entering device ID + claim code).

## 5) Confirm online
The device will provision its token automatically and appear online in ControlMyPrint.

## Reset actions
- Hold reset button **10 seconds**: Wi-Fi reset (keeps pairing/token)
- Hold reset button **20 seconds**: factory reset/unpair (clears Wi-Fi and token)
- Triple press within 3 seconds: soft reboot (no settings erased)

After reset, device returns to AP setup mode.
